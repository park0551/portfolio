About my work

I started out as a scribbler, then an illustrator, and now a designer. I love a good mix of rough, hand-drawn elements and crisp, computer-generated shapes. Not too cookie-cutter, but not too chaotic!

At first I wasn't sure if I would enjoy things like vectors, clean-cut shapes, and coding - but I realized quickly that I really enjoyed discovering the crossover between illustration and design. It's awesome to see certain things come together.

(Making this website just so happened to combine these two art disciplines very well!)
